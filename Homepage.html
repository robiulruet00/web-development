Welcome To KGC

Kurigram Govt. College is situated in the city center, adjacent to Kurigram Govt. School and is very near the Judge Court and DC Office.Established in 1961 and within a short period after establishment, the college became one of the main centers of higher education for the inhabitants of the region. Kurigram Govt. College offers HSC, bachelor and honours degree courses in 14 disciplines since 1997. It also offers masters courses in 9 disciplines under the affiliation of National University. The daily affairs of the college are run on the basis of guidelines prescribed by the Ministry of Education. In spite of its limitations Kurigram Govt. College carries on the heritage of its glory and works persistently in improving the quality of education
